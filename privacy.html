Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 24, 2025  ·  Last Updated: May 24, 2025

Thank you for playing SERPENTIS Wild Kingdom ("the App", "we", "our"). Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information to operate the game and serve advertising. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information unless you provide it voluntarily.

a) Information You Provide

  • Player Nickname: The in-game name you enter is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to our leaderboard via Supabase (an open-source backend). It is not linked to your real identity.

b) Automatically Collected Information

  • Game Data: Your high score, kill count, and rank are stored in Supabase to power the global leaderboard. This data is not linked to your personal identity.
  • Device Identifiers: Advertising services may collect your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) to serve personalized ads, subject to your App Tracking Transparency (ATT) consent.
  • Usage Data: General app interaction data (e.g., ad clicks, session length) may be collected by third-party advertising SDKs.
  • Crash Data: Anonymous crash reports may be collected to improve stability.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob — Serves rewarded advertisements to free users. May collect device identifiers and usage data. Google Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — Powers the global leaderboard. Stores anonymous game scores. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit — Processes in-app purchases. Apple handles all payment data. Apple Privacy Policy

3. Advertising and Tracking (AdMob)

Free users may see rewarded advertisements powered by Google AdMob. By default, we request your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before using your advertising identifier for personalized ads. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will be non-personalized.

AdMob may collect the following data types for advertising purposes:

  • Coarse location (derived from IP address)
  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA)
  • Product interaction (ad views, clicks)
  • Crash and diagnostic data

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time in your device settings: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

4. In-App Purchases

SERPENTIS PRO is available as a one-time, non-consumable in-app purchase processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. All billing is handled by Apple. Purchases are tied to your Apple ID and can be restored across your devices at any time.

5. Data Retention

Leaderboard data (anonymous nickname + score) is retained indefinitely to power the global rankings. You may request deletion by contacting us. Local game data is stored only on your device and is removed when you uninstall the App.

6. Children's Privacy

SERPENTIS Wild Kingdom is rated 4+ by Apple. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data we collect. Leaderboard data transmitted to Supabase is encrypted in transit via HTTPS. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties beyond what is described in this policy.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • GDPR (EU/UK): Right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data.
  • CCPA (California): Right to know what personal information is collected and to opt out of sale (we do not sell personal information).

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version here with the revised effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
